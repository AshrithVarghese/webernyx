FAQ

Common Questions

Are these prices fixed or can they change?

Starter and Professional are fixed prices. The Web App plan is scoped after a discovery call to give you an accurate, honest quote.

Do you offer payment in installments?

Yes. We typically work on a 50% upfront and 50% on delivery basis. Custom arrangements can be discussed for larger projects.

What's included in "support"?

Bug fixes, minor content updates, performance checks, and technical advice. Major new features are scoped separately.

Do you handle hosting and domain setup?

We help you set up and configure hosting (Vercel, AWS, etc.) and DNS. Hosting costs are separate and paid to the provider.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely. Many clients start with Starter and upgrade as their business grows. We build with that transition in mind.

What if I'm not happy with the result?

We work iteratively with client feedback built into every phase. You review and approve before we move forward — no surprises at launch.
